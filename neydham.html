The tranquil Neytham

Tranquility lies where the whispers of nature meet the silence of the soul. - Unknown

The Neytham trek in West Sikkim is a hidden gem for adventurers, offering tranquility and stunning landscapes. This offbeat trail winds through dense forests, serene meadows, and charming villages, with breathtaking views of snow-clad peaks. Untouched by mass tourism, Neytham retains its pristine beauty, making it ideal for trekkers and nature lovers seeking a deeper connection with nature and highland life.
