Understanding Chatbots

What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a software application that can simulate conversation with users using artificial intelligence (AI) and predefined responses.

Types of Chatbots

Chatbots can be rule-based, AI-powered, or hybrid, depending on their complexity and ability to learn from interactions.

Benefits of Using Chatbots

Chatbots improve customer service, automate tasks, and provide 24/7 support, reducing costs and enhancing user experience.
