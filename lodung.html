Lodung River

Rivers know this: there is no hurry. We shall get there someday. - A.A. Milne

Fed by the glacial waters of Kanchenjunga, this river boasts breathtaking clarity, revealing a vibrant mosaic of colorful stones beneath its surface. Its cool, pure flow, untouched by pollution, reflects the pristine beauty of the Himalayas, with sunlight dancing across the ripples to create mesmerizing patterns and rainbows.
